City of Character, Heroes Dinner

The City of Character, Hamilton, Ohio and the Character Council of Greater Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky are honoring exemplary citizens from the Ohio, Kentucky, and Indiana area, who demonstrate outstanding character that clearly connects to our core values. Winning adults representing our four pillars of Education, Business, Faith, and Community will be recognized at the “Heroes of Character” Annual Celebration Dinner on March 15, 2017 at the Courtyard by Marriott in Hamilton.

Anyone may nominate a "Hero" using the form below. Be sure to describe actual examples of his or her exemplary behavior and how the individual's behaviors and dispositions show the Character Qualities in action. Also, please describe obstacles the person has faced, and how they have coped, to illustrate their Character in action. A completed nomination form is required for each person nominated.
